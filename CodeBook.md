# Code Book

This code book summarizes the resulting data fields in `tidy_data.txt`.

## Identifiers

* `subject` - The ID of the test subject
* `activity` - The type of activity performed when the corresponding measurements were taken

## Activity Labels

* `WALKING` (value `1`): subject was walking during the test
* `WALKING_UPSTAIRS` (value `2`): subject was walking up a staircase during the test
* `WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS` (value `3`): subject was walking down a staircase during the test
* `SITTING` (value `4`): subject was sitting during the test
* `STANDING` (value `5`): subject was standing during the test
* `LAYING` (value `6`): subject was laying down during the test

## Measurement Features Naming

* Prefix - `t` denotes time, `f` denotes frequency domain signals
* The acceleration signal is separated into body and gravity acceleration signals - `BodyAcc` and `GravityAcc`
* Body linear acceleration and angular velocity derived in time to obtain Jerk signals - `BodyAccJerk` and `BodyGyroJerk`
* Magnitude of these three-dimensional signals calculated using the Euclidean norm - denoted by `Mag`
* `Mean` - Mean value
* `Std` - Standard deviation
* Suffix - `XYZ` is used to denote 3-axial signals in the X, Y and Z directions
